PicPuzzle App Support

Welcome to the support page for PicPuzzle! Here you’ll find answers to common questions, troubleshooting tips, and ways to contact us for help.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create a puzzle?
    Tap the image selection button on the home screen, choose a photo from your device, then select your desired grid size (3x3, 4x4, or 5x5) and start playing!
  • Can I use any photo?
    Yes! You can select any image from your device’s gallery.
  • How do I reset the puzzle?
    Tap the reload button (⟳) in the top right corner of the puzzle screen to reshuffle the pieces.
  • Is PicPuzzle available offline?
    Yes, you can play PicPuzzle without an internet connection.
  • Which devices are supported?
    PicPuzzle works on Android, iOS, Windows, and web browsers.

Troubleshooting

  • The app won’t open my photo.
    Make sure PicPuzzle has permission to access your device’s photos. Check your device settings and grant access if needed.
  • The puzzle pieces are blank.
    Try selecting a different photo or restarting the app. If the issue persists, contact support below.
  • I found a bug or have a suggestion.
    We appreciate your feedback! Please contact us using the information below.
